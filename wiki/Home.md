# testid-automation Wiki

Welcome! This is the full documentation for `testid-automation` — a build-time toolchain that gives your Angular components stable, readable `data-testid` attributes and keeps them that way across refactorings.

If you're new here, start with [Features](Features) to see what it does, then jump to [Installation](Installation) and the [Quick Start](Quick-Start) to try it out.
